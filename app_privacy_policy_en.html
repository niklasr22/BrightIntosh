Effective Date: 27.07.2023

BrightIntosh ("the App") is dedicated to protecting your privacy. We want to assure you that the App does not collect any data from its users, and it does not connect to the internet in any way. Your personal information remains entirely secure and private when using our App.

Data Collection and Usage:

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or personally identifiable information (PII) from our users. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and we have designed the App to operate entirely offline to ensure that no data is collected.

The App does not have any internet connectivity features. It does not communicate with external servers, and it does not require an internet connection to function. You can use the App with confidence, knowing that no data is being sent or received over the internet.
