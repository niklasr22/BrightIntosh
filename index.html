BrightIntosh

About

BrightIntosh enables your MacBook Pro® M1 (or newer) to use the increased brightness (1000 nits) of its Liquid Retina XDR® display anytime. By default this is only possible when displaying HDR content.

Compatibility

MacBook Pro

  • M1 Pro, M1 Max (2021)
  • M2 Pro, M2 Max (2023)
  • M3, M3 Pro, M3 Max (2023)
