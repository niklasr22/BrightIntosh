How it works

  1. BrightIntosh activates full HDR brightness on your MacBook Pro by creating a tiny 1x1 pixel overlay with the brightest HDR color, prompting macOS to enable maximum screen brightness.
  2. This pixel is discreetly hidden behind the rounded corner of your MacBook Pro's display, making it invisible.
  3. Finally, BrightIntosh adjusts the screen's gamma values, enhancing the brightness across all content displayed.
